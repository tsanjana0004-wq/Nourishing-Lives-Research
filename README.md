**NOURISHING LIVES**
**Project Overview**: This project was developed as part of my academic project.The goal was to create a bridge between food donors and animal shelters to reduce food waste and support animal welfare.
**My Individual Contributions**: This was a collaboration effort, my work was focused on the research and design of the system,
**Literature Survey & Data Collection** : I conducted a survey involving 89 participants to understand the food diposal habit. I analyzed the data to identify the barriers preventing people from donating food to animals.
**Existing System Analysis** : I researched current waste management models and their limitations,most systems are only for human charity.There isn't a central way to food donations specifically for animal shelters,which leading alot of food going to waste instead of helping animals.
**System Logic & Design** : Idefined the requirements for how the platform should work organizing how donors,shelters,and admins would interact.
**Documentation**: I was responsible for bringing all the research and technical logic together into the final project repor ensuring the complex data was explained in a simple and clear way.
